About Us

Ted's Meat Market is an old time meat market with the personal touch of your local butcher. We have been located in Saginaw, MI since 1987 and offer a unique shopping experience for customers seeking top-quality meats. Customers enjoy personal service and expert advice on cooking, grilling and recipe planning. Our goal is to provide meats that taste better and cost less than our competitors. From the time you walk in we want you to feel like family and be treated even better.
